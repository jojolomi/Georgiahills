Martvili Canyon: Emerald Waters

Discover the hidden gem of Western Georgia. Martvili Canyon offers a surreal experience with its emerald green waters, waterfalls, and white limestone cliffs.

The highlight of any trip here is a boat ride through the stunning gorges. Historically, this was a bathing place for the Dadiani noble family. Today, it stands as one of the most photogenic spots in the country.
