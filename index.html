Essential Info for Tourists

Visa Requirements

Citizens of GCC countries (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain) enjoy a visa-free regime for up to 1 year. Just pack and fly!

Halal Food

Halal food is widely available in Tbilisi, especially in the Marjanishvili area. Our drivers know the best authentic spots.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April-June) and Autumn (September-October) offer pleasant weather. For snow lovers, December to February is perfect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay in advance?

No, payment is strictly upon arrival. You can pay the driver in cash (GEL, USD, EUR) when you meet.

Do drivers speak Arabic?

Yes! We have professional drivers who speak Arabic, as well as English and Russian. Please mention this in your booking notes.

Is fuel included in the price?

Yes, for all tours with a driver, fuel is completely included in your quote. For self-drive rentals, you receive the car full and return it full.
