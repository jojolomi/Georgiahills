Our Story

More than drivers.
We are your Georgian hosts.

At Georgia Hills, we believe that the best way to see a country is through the eyes of a local friend. Founded by a team of passionate travel experts, we set out to change the standard of private transport in Georgia.

We noticed that too many visitors were treated as just another fare. We wanted to offer something different: genuine care. From the moment you land in Tbilisi to your final departure, we are dedicated to your comfort, safety, and enjoyment.

Whether it's finding the perfect mountain view, recommending a hidden restaurant, or simply ensuring a smooth ride for your sleeping children, we are here for you. We don't just drive; we host.
