Is Georgia Safe for Travelers?

Quick, practical safety guidance for city and mountain travel.

General Safety

Tourist areas are generally safe, but standard travel awareness always helps.

Transport Safety

Use known drivers, confirm routes, and avoid late-night ad-hoc transport in unfamiliar areas.

Mountain Roads

Weather changes quickly; experienced drivers are recommended for highland routes.

Family Travel

Plan restroom/meal stops, keep offline maps, and share itinerary with your contact.
