The Charm of Kakheti

Signagi is one of Georgia's most charming towns, with its red rooftops, wooden balconies, and cobblestone streets reminiscent of Tuscany.

Walk along the 18th-century defensive walls for an incredible panorama of the Caucasus Mountains. Visit the Bodbe Monastery (burial place of St. Nino) nearby, and enjoy wine tasting in the "Cradle of Wine".

Must-See Highlights

City Walls

Walk the longest preserved defensive wall in Georgia with 23 towers.

Bodbe Monastery

Holy pilgrimage site where St. Nino is buried, with beautiful gardens.

Wine Tasting

Taste exquisite local wines like Saperavi and Rkatsiteli.

Alazani Valley

Spectacular panoramic views of the valley and Great Caucasus.
