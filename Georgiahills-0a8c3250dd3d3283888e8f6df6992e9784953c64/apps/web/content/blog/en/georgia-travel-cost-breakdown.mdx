# Georgia Travel Cost Breakdown 2026

> **Verified fact:** According to Numbeo's 2024 Cost of Living Index, Tbilisi is among the top 20 most affordable capital cities in Europe. A mid-range restaurant meal costs $8–$15 per person. ([Source: Numbeo Tbilisi Cost of Living](https://www.numbeo.com/cost-of-living/in/Tbilisi))

The smartest way to budget a Georgia trip is to break it down by category, not use a single total estimate.

## 1. Accommodation

| Tier | Price per night (double room) |
|---|---|
| Budget guesthouse | $30–$50 |
| Mid-range hotel | $70–$130 |
| Premium/boutique | $150–$250+ |

**Tip:** Location matters as much as price. A well-located mid-range hotel usually saves more in transport costs than a budget hotel far from the center.

## 2. Private Transport

For families, a dedicated private driver for the full trip is often more cost-effective than booking transport day-by-day.

| Service | Approximate Price |
|---|---|
| Airport transfer (1-way) | $15–$25 |
| Full day in Tbilisi | $50–$70 |
| Tbilisi → Kazbegi (day trip) | $80–$120 |
| Tbilisi → Batumi (transfer) | $120–$180 |
| 7-day package (all included) | $350–$560 |

## 3. Food and Dining

Georgian cuisine is excellent and affordable. Budget roughly:

- Casual local restaurant: $6–$10 per person
- Mid-range restaurant: $10–$18 per person
- Fine dining: $20–$35 per person

A family of 4, eating two sit-down meals per day: approximately $60–$90/day.

## 4. Activities and Entrance Fees

Most outdoor activities in Georgia are free (walking, viewpoints, nature). Entrance fees for key sites:

- Narikala Fortress area: free
- Mtatsminda Park (funicular): ~$3/person
- Botanical Garden Batumi: ~$4/person
- Gergeti Church hike: free

Budget $150–$300 for 7 days of activities for a family of 4.

## 5. Sample 7-Day Budget (Family of 4)

| Category | Budget | Mid | Premium |
|---|---|---|---|
| Accommodation | $280 | $630 | $1,050 |
| Transport | $350 | $450 | $560 |
| Food | $400 | $500 | $650 |
| Activities | $100 | $180 | $300 |
| Contingency (10%) | $115 | $176 | $256 |
| **Total** | **~$1,245** | **~$1,936** | **~$2,816** |

## Smart Saving Tips

1. **Book accommodation and transport early** — especially in summer.
2. **Stick to 2–3 cities** — more cities = more transfer costs.
3. **Use one private driver for the whole trip** — avoids daily price negotiation.
4. **Eat lunch at local restaurants** — quality is high and prices are very reasonable.
5. **Plan activities in advance** — paid guided tours are optional, most key sites are self-guided.
