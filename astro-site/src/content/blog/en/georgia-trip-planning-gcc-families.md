## 8-Week Booking Timeline

Planning a Georgia family trip is straightforward when done in the right sequence.

**8 weeks before travel:**
- Check passport validity (6 months minimum).
- Search and book flights — FlyDubai, Qatar Airways, and Jazeera Airways have the best Gulf–Tbilisi fares.
- Choose travel dates based on [seasonal guide](/blog/en/best-time-to-visit-georgia.html).

**6 weeks before:**
- Book accommodation (hotels in Tbilisi fill quickly in summer).
- Contact Georgia Hills to confirm driver and vehicle type.
- Plan rough itinerary: how many days per city?

**4 weeks before:**
- Finalise Georgia Hills booking and receive WhatsApp confirmation.
- Purchase travel insurance (most Gulf insurers cover Georgia).
- Download offline maps (Maps.me or Google Maps offline).

**1 week before:**
- Download prayer time app with Georgia/Tbilisi settings.
- Notify your bank of travel to avoid card blocks.
- Pack power adaptor (Georgia uses EU Type C/F plugs, 220V).

## Visa Requirements

GCC nationals enjoy **visa-free access** to Georgia for up to 90 days. This applies to:
- Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦
- UAE 🇦🇪
- Kuwait 🇰🇼
- Qatar 🇶🇦
- Bahrain 🇧🇭
- Oman 🇴🇲

Simply present your passport at immigration. No e-visa or prior registration required.

## What to Pack

- **Modest clothing** for church visits (shoulders and knees covered — a scarf or light jacket works).
- **Comfortable walking shoes** for Tbilisi's cobblestone streets.
- **Layer system** for Kazbegi (even in summer, mountain temperatures can drop to 8–12°C at night).
- **Prayer mat and Qibla compass** — hotels don't always have these.
- **Power adaptor** (Type C/F, 220V — UAE/Saudi plugs won't fit Georgian outlets).
