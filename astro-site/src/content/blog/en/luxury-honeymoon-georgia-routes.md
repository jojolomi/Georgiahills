## Why Georgia for Your Honeymoon

Georgia delivers luxury-level experiences at two-thirds the price of comparable European destinations. The combination of Old Tbilisi's romance, Kazbegi's dramatic scenery, and Batumi's coastal atmosphere creates a honeymoon unlike anywhere else in the region.

## Romantic Route 1: The Classic (7 Nights)

**Tbilisi 3 nights → Kakheti wine region 1 night → Kazbegi 2 nights → Tbilisi 1 night**

- Arrive Tbilisi; check into a boutique Old Town hotel with a balcony view of Narikala.
- Private sunset walk through Abanotubani (sulfur bath district) by candlelight.
- Wine tasting in the Alazani Valley at a family winery.
- Sunrise at Gergeti Trinity Church before other tourists arrive.
- Return to Tbilisi for a final dinner at a rooftop restaurant.

## Romantic Route 2: Sea and Mountains (8 Nights)

**Tbilisi 2 nights → Kazbegi 2 nights → Tbilisi 1 night → Batumi 3 nights**

- Tbilisi arrival, Old Town exploration.
- Mountain escape to Kazbegi — mountain cabin with fireplace.
- Return south to Batumi via the Rikoti Pass (stunning scenery).
- Three nights on the Black Sea: Boulevard walks, seafood dinners, dolphin boat tour option.

## Accommodation Highlights

### Tbilisi
- **Stamba Hotel** — Vake district; industrial-chic design, spa, rooftop bar.
- **Rooms Hotel Tbilisi** — Vera; minimalist design with garden courtyard.
- **Fabrika Tbilisi Hostel Complex** — Chugureti; creative hub with high-end private rooms.

### Kazbegi
- **Rooms Hotel Kazbegi** — Glass-walled design with Gergeti Trinity Church in frame.
- **Stephantsminda Luxury Chalets** — Private mountain chalets for 2.

### Batumi
- **Sheraton Batumi** — Seafront; full spa, rooftop pool.
- **Hilton Batumi** — Central Boulevard location.
