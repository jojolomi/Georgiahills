# Georgia 7-Day Family Itinerary

This itinerary balances city exploration, mountain scenery, and rest-friendly pacing — designed for GCC families visiting Georgia for the first time.

> **Verified fact:** Georgia welcomed over 7.1 million international visitors in 2023, making it one of the fastest-growing tourism destinations in the Caucasus. ([Source: Georgian National Tourism Administration](https://gnta.ge/statistics/))

## Day 1 — Arrival in Tbilisi

- Private airport transfer to hotel.
- Rest and light evening walk near your accommodation.

The goal on day one is recovery, not sightseeing. A flexible first evening sets the right pace for the rest of the trip.

## Day 2 — Historic Tbilisi

- Old Tbilisi (Abanotubani, Peace Bridge, Narikala Fortress views).
- Panoramic photo stop for the family.
- Relaxed dinner in the old town.

Split the day into a morning walk and an afternoon break — especially important with kids.

## Day 3 — Modern Tbilisi and Rustaveli

- Rustaveli Avenue, Dry Bridge Market, Mtatsminda Park.
- Free time for shopping and restaurant choice.

This day is flexible — shorten it if you want to head toward the mountains a day earlier.

## Day 4 — Transfer to Kazbegi (Early Start)

- Depart before 8:00 AM for a 2.5–3 hour scenic drive.
- Stops at Georgian Military Highway viewpoints.
- Arrive Kazbegi early afternoon — rest at hotel.

> **Expert note:** "Leaving Tbilisi early avoids road congestion and gives the family a full afternoon in Kazbegi. The Jvari Pass viewpoint mid-route is a must-stop for photos." — Georgia Hills Team

## Day 5 — Kazbegi Mountains

- Gergeti Trinity Church hike or vehicle ascent (weather permitting).
- Village walk and mountain views.
- Quiet family dinner at altitude.

Keep activities light at altitude — the mountain air tires children faster than expected.

## Day 6 — Transfer to Batumi

- ~4–5 hour drive with comfort breaks.
- Arrive Batumi early evening.
- Short Batumi Boulevard walk.

## Day 7 — Batumi and Departure

- Botanical Garden or Boulevard morning walk.
- Free time for shopping or beach.
- Airport transfer based on your flight time.

## Tips for GCC Families

- No visa required for Saudi, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman passport holders.
- Book accommodation 4–6 weeks ahead in summer to secure the best locations.
- A single private driver for the full 7 days simplifies logistics significantly.
- Pack one warm layer per person — Kazbegi can be 10°C cooler than Tbilisi.
