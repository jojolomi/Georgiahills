The Jewel of Samegrelo

Martvili Canyon is a hidden gem that offers a surreal experience with its emerald green waters, cascading 70-meter waterfalls, and white limestone cliffs covered in moss.

The highlight of any trip here is a boat ride through the stunning gorges. Historically, this was a bathing place for the Dadiani noble family. Today, it stands as one of the most photogenic and peaceful spots in the country.

Must-See Highlights

Boat Trip

Navigate the calm green waters of the Abasha river by boat.

Walking Trails

700m circular hiking trail with scenic bridges and viewing platforms.

Dadiani Palace

Historical museum and summer residence of Mingrelian princes nearby.

Okatse Canyon

Famous hanging walkway canyon often visited on the same trip.
