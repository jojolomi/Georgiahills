Georgia Travel Guide

Practical information for a smooth, safe and enjoyable trip.

Visa

Most GCC travelers can enter Georgia visa-free (subject to latest border policy).

Best Time

Spring and autumn for mild weather; winter for snow resorts.

Halal Food

Many halal restaurants are available in central Tbilisi areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay in advance?

No. Payment can be made at trip start unless otherwise agreed.

Can I request an Arabic-speaking driver?

Yes, subject to availability. Mention this in your request.

Are fuel and parking included?

Included for driver service unless clearly excluded in custom quote.
