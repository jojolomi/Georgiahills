# GCC Micro-Influencer Shortlist (Fam-Trip)

## Selection Criteria
- Audience concentration in Saudi/UAE/Qatar/Kuwait/Bahrain/Oman
- Travel/family/lifestyle relevance
- Arabic-first storytelling quality
- Engagement rate > follower vanity

## Candidate List (3-6)
1. Family travel creator (Saudi) - focus on kid-friendly itineraries and practical costs.
2. Luxury travel creator (UAE) - focus on premium transport and VIP route quality.
3. Couples/honeymoon creator (Qatar) - focus on scenic and privacy-focused experiences.
4. Halal lifestyle creator (Kuwait) - focus on halal-friendly and prayer-aware planning.
5. Adventure travel creator (Oman) - focus on Kazbegi/Gudauri/Svaneti route storytelling.
6. Women travel creator (Bahrain) - focus on safety, comfort, and women-friendly services.

## Brief Requirements
- 3 short videos + 1 long reel + 5 story sets
- Mention route names (Tbilisi, Batumi, Kazbegi, Gudauri, Svaneti, Kakheti)
- Include direct CTA to WhatsApp booking link
- Include at least one Arabic keyword in caption and spoken narration
