# GCC Micro-Influencer Shortlist (Fam-Trip)

## Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Minimum Threshold |
|---|---|
| Audience in GCC countries (SA/UAE/QA/KW/BH/OM) | ≥ 60% of audience |
| Engagement rate | ≥ 3.5% on travel content |
| Followers | 15K–500K (micro-macro range) |
| Content language | Arabic primary or bilingual Arabic/English |
| Niche relevance | Family / halal / budget / adventure travel |
| Past geo coverage | Previous Caucasus, Turkey, or SE Asia trips preferred |

---

## Candidate Profiles (6 Shortlisted Slots)

### Slot 1: Family Travel Creator — Saudi Arabia
- **Focus:** Kid-friendly itineraries and practical daily costs
- **Target audience:** Saudi families, 2–4 children, 30–45 age range
- **Platform:** Instagram + TikTok
- **Ideal post type:** Day-in-the-life reel in Kazbegi, family restaurant hunt in Tbilisi
- **Key message:** "You can do Georgia with kids — here's the full real plan"
- **CTA:** Direct to WhatsApp family package booking
- **Audience target:** 50K–200K followers with ≥4% engagement

### Slot 2: Luxury / VIP Creator — UAE
- **Focus:** Premium transport, scenic views, VIP route quality
- **Target audience:** UAE couples and high-income professionals
- **Platform:** Instagram + YouTube Shorts
- **Ideal post type:** Luxury SUV on Georgian Military Highway, boutique hotel in Tbilisi
- **Key message:** "Georgia for those who don't compromise on comfort"
- **CTA:** Direct to luxury package booking
- **Audience target:** 30K–150K followers, strong engagement from UAE

### Slot 3: Couples / Honeymoon Creator — Qatar
- **Focus:** Romantic routes, scenic backdrop, privacy-focused experiences
- **Target audience:** Qatari and Gulf newlyweds and engaged couples
- **Platform:** Instagram + TikTok
- **Ideal post type:** Gergeti Trinity Church golden hour, couple walk on Batumi boulevard
- **Key message:** "Georgia is the hidden honeymoon destination from the Gulf"
- **CTA:** Honeymoon package enquiry via WhatsApp
- **Audience target:** 20K–100K followers, bilingual content

### Slot 4: Halal Lifestyle Creator — Kuwait
- **Focus:** Halal food discovery, prayer stop planning, Muslim-friendly travel
- **Target audience:** Kuwaiti families and individuals conscious of halal travel
- **Platform:** Instagram + YouTube
- **Ideal post type:** Halal restaurant tour in Tbilisi, mosque visit, prayer in the mountains
- **Key message:** "Georgia works for halal travel — if you plan it right"
- **CTA:** Family halal package booking
- **Audience target:** 25K–120K followers with strong Kuwait/Gulf audience

### Slot 5: Adventure Travel Creator — Oman / UAE
- **Focus:** Kazbegi mountains, Gudauri terrain, Svaneti trekking routes
- **Target audience:** Active GCC travelers, 25–40, male-leaning
- **Platform:** YouTube + Instagram
- **Ideal post type:** Kazbegi mountain run, Gudauri drive with drone footage
- **Key message:** "The closest mountain adventure from the Gulf you don't know about yet"
- **CTA:** Book custom adventure package
- **Audience target:** 10K–80K followers, high engagement on adventure content

### Slot 6: Women Travel Creator — Bahrain / Saudi Arabia
- **Focus:** Safety, comfort, women-friendly services, group travel
- **Target audience:** GCC women, 25–40, solo or group travelers
- **Platform:** Instagram + Podcast
- **Ideal post type:** Solo walk in Tbilisi old town, women group experience, safety tips vlog
- **Key message:** "Georgia is safe, respectful, and worth it — from a Gulf woman's perspective"
- **CTA:** Women group package enquiry
- **Audience target:** 15K–100K followers, Arabic-speaking community

## Content Requirements (Brief)

All creators must:
1. Mention at least 2 route names from: Tbilisi, Batumi, Kazbegi, Gudauri, Svaneti, Kakheti
2. Include at least one Arabic keyword in captions (e.g., "سائق خاص في جورجيا", "رحلة عائلية إلى جورجيا")
3. Tag @georgiahills (or agreed handle) in at least 1 post
4. Include direct WhatsApp CTA in at least 1 story or bio link
5. NOT use stock footage — all content must be shot during the fam-trip
6. Disclose partnership per platform guidelines (`#ad` / `#sponsored` where required)

---

## Outreach Priority

1. Identify 3 confirmed candidates (Slots 1, 4, 6 have highest search alignment)
2. Send brief and agreement via DM/email
3. Confirm travel dates and logistics (travel covered by Georgia Hills)
4. Review content before publishing (24h review window)
5. Track performance for 30 days post-publish: impressions, saves, WhatsApp clicks
