Top 5 Must-Try Georgian Dishes

A practical guide for first-time visitors and family travelers.

1) Khinkali

Traditional dumplings served with meat or mushroom fillings. Best eaten hot and fresh.

2) Khachapuri

Georgia’s iconic cheese bread. Adjarian style is the most popular among tourists.

3) Mtsvadi

Charcoal-grilled skewers often served with onion and pomegranate.

4) Lobio

Seasoned bean stew, a great vegetarian option.

5) Pkhali

Walnut-based vegetable starters, commonly served as appetizer plates.
