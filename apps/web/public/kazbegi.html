The High Caucasus

Stepantsminda, commonly known as Kazbegi, is more than just a destination; it's an experience of raw nature. Located at the foot of Mount Kazbek (5,054m), this region offers some of the most dramatic landscapes in Europe.

The journey itself is a highlight. Driving north from Tbilisi along the Georgian Military Highway, you'll pass the turquoise waters of the Zhinvali Reservoir and the historic Ananuri Fortress before climbing the Cross Pass to reach the clouds.
