Privacy Policy

Georgia Hills collects only the information needed to respond to requests and organize transportation services, including name, phone number, trip details, and communication preferences.

We use your information to provide quotes, confirm bookings, coordinate drivers, and handle customer support. We do not sell your personal data.

Your details may be shared only with operational partners directly involved in your booking (for example, an assigned driver) and only when required to deliver the service.

If you contact us through WhatsApp, email, or web forms, those channels may process data under their own policies. You can request correction or deletion of your data by contacting info@georgiahills.com.
