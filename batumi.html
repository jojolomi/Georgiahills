The Las Vegas of the Black Sea

Batumi is a vibrant seaside city on the Black Sea coast and the capital of Adjara. Known for its eclectic modern architecture, bustling boulevard, and subtropical climate, it offers a stark contrast to the historical depth of Tbilisi.

Stroll through the famous Botanical Garden, relax on the pebbly beaches, or enjoy the nightlife along the promenade. Batumi is the perfect summer getaway for those seeking relaxation and entertainment.

Must-See Highlights

Ali & Nino Statue

Moving metal sculpture of eternal love by the sea.

Batumi Boulevard

7km seaside promenade with fountains & cafès.

Botanical Garden

One of the largest and most diverse gardens in the world.

Alphabetic Tower

130m high tower dedicated to Georgian script.
